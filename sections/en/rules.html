Reglamento Medieval

A. Chat & Medieval composure:

  1. Always follow your common sense.
  2. Be a gentleman (Respectful, and always looking forward to preserve your honor).
  3. Any kind of Spam or Flood in chat is strictly forbidden.
  4. Rule Nº3 includes, but is not limited to: Giving out the ip of other servers as a way of advertising , sending the same or similar messages repeatedly in chat, sharing malicious links (pron, gore, etc..), among others.
  5. Racial and xenophobic insults are not tolerated.
  6. Keep a calm atmosphere in chat.
  7. Harassing or repeatedly bothering other players is not tolerated.
  8. Suicide encouragement is absolutely not legal and will be severely punished.
  9. Staff impersonation is not allowed (To intentionally pretend being part of the server staff).

B. Client Modifications:

❖ Forbidden modifications:

  1. Any kind of “mining-assistance” tools. I.e: X-Ray, texture packs which reveal ores locations, etc...
  2. Minimaps that may reveal the location of players, chests, etc...
  3. Any kind of unfair advantage in general: Fly, Velocity, Reach, etc...

❖ Allowed modifications:

  1. Texture packs used to improve gameplay.
  2. Mods like OptiFine.

C. Combat Policy (PvP):

  1. On the server, a deadline will be set. Passed it's limit, the medieval players will move from being on the armed peace season to be entering a new war, desperation and mundial catastrophe season.
  2. The pvp before war season's beggining is allowed, yet it's encouraged to users that they pact limits and treats between empires before getting involved in a conflict.
  3. The pvp within war season is absolutely permitted. In fact, we encourage players to take part in the mundial crisis, and to promote their empire's arrival to the top of the medieval history.

D. Ownership of properties:

  1. Every player is allowed to claim 2500 blocks by default.
  2. In war periods, claim protection will be turned off, allowing other players to destroy your terrains.
  3. The maximum amount of claim blocks you may own is 5000.

E. Clans Normative:

❖ Clans creation:

  1. Any clan names like "fljwouhwifdsg" will be eliminated. Please chose an appropriate name.
  2. Chat & Medieval composure ruleset applies to clan names as well, so read carefully.
  3. We encourage players to choose medieval names for their clans. I.e: España".

❖ Clans internal rules:

  1. The rules and interactions within clans are completely based on trust and loyalty.
  2. Every clan is responsible of any action taken by any of it's members.
